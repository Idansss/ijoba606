Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our consultant marketplace.

How are consultants vetted? expand_more
All verified consultants go through a rigorous screening process. We verify their professional credentials, past experience, and regulatory standing with relevant Nigerian tax authorities and professional bodies.
What are the typical rates for a consultation? expand_more
Rates vary depending on the consultant's experience and the complexity of your needs. Typical hourly rates range from ₦15,000 to ₦100,000+. You can see clear pricing on every consultant's profile before booking.
Can I become a consultant on the platform? expand_more
Yes! If you are a certified tax professional, accountant, or financial advisor, we welcome you to apply. Click the "Become a Consultant" button in the hero section to start your application and verification process.
How do payments work? expand_more
Payments are handled securely through your IJOBA Wallet. Funds are held in escrow when you book a session and are only released to the consultant once the consultation is successfully completed.
